Interpretation

The correlation coefficient between BCG vaccination coverage and TB incidence in 2020 is -0.171.

Based on the analysis of data from 166 countries in 2020:

  1. The average TB incidence rate is 71.1 cases per 100,000 population (median: 39.5)
  2. The average BCG vaccination coverage is 88.7% (median: 93%)
  3. The correlation coefficient (-0.171) suggests a negative relationship between BCG vaccination coverage and TB incidence rates

The choropleth maps reveal clear geographical patterns: - Higher TB incidence rates are generally observed in parts of Africa and Asia - BCG vaccination coverage is relatively high across most of the developing world - The negative correlation suggests that countries with higher vaccination rates tend to have lower TB incidence, though other factors likely play important roles in this relationship
